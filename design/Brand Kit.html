01 — thesis

a life, indexed.

three principles. every surface obeys them. when a choice is ambiguous, this is the tiebreaker.

i.

plainspoken over clever

it's a life-log, not a productivity tool. the words on the surface should be the words you'd say out loud.

ii.

literary over technical

even though the data is structured. read like a journal entry, not a database row.

iii.

ongoing over finished

a life is never done. the brand should never feel sealed. leave room for one more entry below the last.

02 — the mark

two dots, asymmetric.

a timeline glyph disguised as syntax. the smallest possible timeline — two points, the eye fills in the line. punctuation native to both readers.

a.

upper dot — the present

larger. rendered in the personal accent. closest to the reader, most vivid. the latest entry sits here.

b.

lower dot — the past

smaller. rendered in ink. recedes downward. the eye fills the line between them: the timeline is implied, never drawn.

c.

the colon, weaponised

a colon is the most semantically loaded punctuation mark — it introduces. 2026: launched logr. mom: 1954–2018. that is the grammar of a log entry.

07 — timeline grammar

the layout primitive.

every page that shows events uses the same vertical hairline. recent entries sit at the top with a larger accent dot; older ones recede. the line is implied — drawn only thin enough to be felt.

10 — don't

the surfaces this brand refuses.

the north star: if a stranger says "is this an ai tool?" — the brand has failed. the duality is discovered, never advertised.

×no clocks, calendars, hourglasses or watches. time is implied, never illustrated.
×no brains, neural networks, sparkles or orbs. this is not an ai brand.
×no chat bubbles or message ui. logr is a document, not a conversation.
×no purple-to-blue saas gradients. no gradients at all.
×no infinity loops, swooshes or abstract motion marks.
×no bracket containers — they signal sealed, finished. a life isn't.
×no emoji in marketing copy. no :). no 🚀. ever.
×no title case. no all caps. lowercase is the house style.
×no shadows, no glows, no rounded buttons. flat surfaces only.
×no "powered by ai." lead with the human. let the ai use be a consequence.
